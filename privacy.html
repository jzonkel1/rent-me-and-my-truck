Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Rent Me and My Truck LLC ("we," "us") operates this website to tell you about our hauling, delivery and removal services in Corpus Christi, Texas and the surrounding area. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Information you send us — when you request an estimate we ask for your name, phone number, the type of job, and any details you choose to share. Email is optional.
  • Basic technical information — like most websites, our hosting provider keeps standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited).

How we use it

  • To reply to your estimate request by call or text and to schedule and perform the work.
  • To keep records of jobs and customer communication.

By submitting your phone number, you agree that we may call or text you about your request. Message and data rates from your carrier may apply. You can ask us to stop at any time.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us run this site and deliver messages (for example, our form-delivery provider), and only as needed to respond to you, or where the law requires.

Keeping and deleting your information

We keep estimate requests and job records only as long as we reasonably need them for business purposes. If you'd like us to delete the information you sent us, call, text, or email us using the contact details below and we'll take care of it.

Children

This site is intended for adults arranging hauling and removal services. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version on this page with a new effective date.
